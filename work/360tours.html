/ ABOUT

The idea

This client ran a business helping real estate agents market their property photos more effective. They'd recently started offering 360-degree photography as part of their packages and needed an app that could turn those photos into interactive virtual tours.

Since he already had his site running on WordPress, I had to step outside my usual stack a bit. I ended up building it as a WordPress plugin — he uploads a 360 image, places hotspots, and links them to different views.

/ HOW IT WORKS

Hotspot navigation

Each 360° photo is a scene. Scenes are connected by hotspots — interactive markers placed within the panorama that trigger a transition to another scene when clicked. The result is a self-contained virtual tour that feels spatial, even though it's just a series of linked images.

/ HIGHLIGHTS

What's in it

  • →360° panorama viewer with pan, zoom, and drag controls
  • →Visual hotspot placement — click to position, link to another scene
  • →Multi-scene tours with seamless transitions between photos
  • →WordPress admin integration — manage tours from the dashboard
  • →Shortcode embed — drop a tour into any post or page
