CLIENT WORK

Gravitybarbers.cz —
opening in weeks, no website, previous dev gone

A brand-new barbershop in Brno needed to be findable and bookable before doors opened. Their previous developer had disappeared. I stepped in and got them live.

/ THE PROBLEM

No web presence, opening day closing in

The owners had just founded the shop and their previous developer had bailed. They needed a site that showed hours, listed services, and made booking obvious — fast. The mission was simple: get online so clients can start booking before the doors open.

/ WHAT I BUILT

A lean site that does what it needs to

Sat with the owners, discussed the vibe, and built. Symfony on the backend, Stimulus and Turbo on the front — lightweight, fast, easy to maintain. Booking integrated with their existing scheduling tool.

  • →Hours, services, location — all immediately visible
  • →Booking tied directly to the barbers' scheduling tool
/ THE RESULT

From zero web presence to Google-indexed and bookable

The shop went from having nothing online to a live, Google-indexed site with booking built in. Clients find them, see the services, and book — no phone calls needed outside of hours. Live since mid-2025 and still running as-is.
