Gravitybarbers.cz —
a barber site, then a redesign

A small business website for a Brno barbershop — clear hours, easy booking, gallery. Then a self-initiated redesign exploring a sharper visual direction.

/ ABOUT

The brief

A newly founded barbershop in Brno needed a website quickly since they had a problem with a previous developer — show the hours, list the services, make booking obvious and easy.

Built fast (in about 2 weeks), kept lean, no CMS overhead. Fully static, edited directly when something changes.

The mission was direct - build a simple website, so we can open as soon as possible and clients can start booking. Sat with the owners, we discussed the overall theme and went with it.

/ REDESIGN

The unsolicited part

After the site went live I kept poking at it — exploring a better feel and a more confident imagery. The redesign isn't deployed (it's the client's call and they're happy with the quick version), but it's the version I'd push.
