About Me

Hey there! I’m Vojta, a developer by day, fighter by night. I’ve always loved solving problems—whether debugging code, or figuring out the best way to land a kick.

I mostly work with TypeScript and Next.js, focusing on building smooth, efficient web apps. For me, coding isn’t just about writing lines of code—it’s about truly understanding how things work under the hood. I don’t just write code that works; I make sure I know why it works.

Beyond coding, I’m all about growth, challenges, and new experiences. If I’m not at my desk, you’ll probably find me training Muay Thai or planning my next adventure.
